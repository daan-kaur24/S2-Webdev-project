Maldives ▼

The Maldives is a luxurious tropical destination known for its crystal-clear waters, white sandy beaches, and overwater villas. It offers a peaceful escape and is ideal for relaxation, honeymoons, and premium vacations.

This package includes a 5-night stay in a beach villa resort with all meals, private beach access, and exciting water activities like snorkeling and diving.

Dubai ▼

Dubai is a modern city famous for its futuristic skyline, luxury lifestyle, and cultural experiences. From Burj Khalifa to desert safaris, it offers a mix of adventure and comfort.

This package includes a 4-night stay, desert safari, city tours, and visits to major attractions like Dubai Mall and Burj Khalifa.

Paris ▼

Paris is known as the City of Love and is famous for its romantic atmosphere, art, fashion, and iconic landmarks such as the Eiffel Tower.

This package includes a 5-night stay, guided tours, and a Seine river cruise, offering a perfect mix of history and modern charm.

Bali ▼

Bali is a tropical paradise known for its beaches, temples, and natural beauty. It is perfect for relaxation as well as adventure activities.

This package includes a 5-night resort stay, temple tours, beach activities, and local cultural experiences.

Switzerland ▼

Switzerland is known for its breathtaking mountain landscapes, scenic lakes, and peaceful environment. It is one of the best destinations for nature lovers and those seeking a calm and refreshing holiday.

This package includes a 6-night stay, mountain excursions, train journeys through the Alps, and guided city tours of Zurich and Lucerne.

Thailand ▼

Thailand is a vibrant destination known for its beaches, nightlife, and cultural heritage. It offers a mix of relaxation, adventure, and entertainment.

This package includes a 5-night stay, island tours, beach activities, and visits to famous locations like Phuket and Bangkok.
